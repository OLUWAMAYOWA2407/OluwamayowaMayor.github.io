What I have To Offer You

"If you torture your Data long enough,
it will confess to anything." -Ronald Coase

Descriptive Data Analytics

I am passionate about using analytic skills to help individuals, companies, organizations, and businesses to provide answers to questions of "what has happened?" over a given period of time by summarizing past Data or a given use case Data to profer actionable solutions to their problems.

Diagnostic Analytics

Having taken a Descriptive step to know what happened over period of time. I also take interest in digging deeper into Data to answer the question of "Why did it happen?", in order to find the cause of the outcome.

Predictive Analytics

I will help to answer the question of "what will happen next?", leveraging on historical Data and trends to predict future outcome like risk assessment and sales forecast.

Prescriptive Analytics

"What should be done about it?" I will help to analyze past events and patterns from given Data or a use case Data to estimate the likelihood of different outcomes in a clear and easy to understand language through explicit visualization and plainn texts.
